OH-Metadata Registry

The Radboud University Oral History Metadata Registry provides an interface to list, correct and add metadata specifications for Oral History transcriptions. Each OH metadata item contains administrative and descriptive information on the ASR transcription. The description of an OH metadata item is defined by the Clarin Component Registry.

Learn more »

Getting started

The OH-Metadata Registry has been designed by the Technical Service Group of the Humanities Lab at the Radboud University Nijmegen. Any researcher is invited to create a log in and maintain his or her own collection of OH metadata items. Collections of one researcher are not visible to other researchers.
