OH-Metadata Registry

The Radboud University Oral History Metadata Registry provides an interface to list, correct and add metadata specifications for Oral History recordings. Each OH metadata item contains administrative and descriptive information about a specific recording (typically an interview). The description of an OH metadata item is defined by the Clarin Component Registry.

Learn more »

Getting started

The OH-Metadata Registry has been designed by the Technical Service Group of the Humanities Lab at the Radboud University Nijmegen. Any researcher is invited to registrate (create an account) and maintain his or her own collection of OH metadata. Collections of one researcher are by default not visible to other researchers, but it is possible to specify a different accessibility level.
